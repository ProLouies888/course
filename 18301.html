Advertising in Marketing Course



The "Marketing Mix" refers to a set of controllable, tactical marketing tools that a company uses to produce a desired response in the target market. Traditionally known as the 4Ps—Product, Price, Place, and Promotion—these elements help businesses plan and execute effective marketing strategies.

In marketing, creating awareness is the crucial first step in building a connection between a brand and its target audience. It involves making potential customers aware of a product, service, or brand through various strategies and channels.



Building brand identity is the process of creating a unique image and personality for a brand in the minds of consumers. It involves defining key elements such as the brand’s name, logo, colors, messaging, and overall tone, all of which work together to communicate what the brand stands for.
